Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Green Thumb is a plant care tracking app developed by Ryan Roark. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Green Thumb is designed with privacy in mind. The app collects only what is necessary to provide its features:

  • Plant data — plant names, photos, species, care history, and notes you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are a Green Thumb Pro subscriber and are signed into iCloud, this data may also be synced to iCloud to enable cross-device access.
  • Plant identification photos — when you use the Identify Plant feature, photos are sent to Plant.id (plant.id), a third-party plant identification service, to identify the plant species. These photos are subject to Plant.id's own privacy policy, which can be found at plant.id.
  • Notification preferences — your notification settings and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All plant data, care history, photos, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Green Thumb does not operate any servers or databases that store your personal information.

iCloud Sync (Pro feature): If you are a Green Thumb Pro subscriber and are signed into iCloud on your device, your plant data may be synced to Apple's iCloud servers via CloudKit. This allows your data to be accessed across your Apple devices. Green Thumb does not have direct access to data stored in iCloud — it is transmitted directly between your device and Apple's servers. iCloud data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy. You can disable iCloud sync at any time by signing out of iCloud or disabling iCloud Drive in your device's Settings app.

iCloud and Apple Services

Green Thumb Pro uses Apple's CloudKit framework to sync your plant data across your devices via iCloud. This data is stored on Apple's servers and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. Green Thumb does not have access to your iCloud data outside of the app. If you choose not to use iCloud sync, all data remains stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Green Thumb uses the following third-party service:

  • Plant.id — used for plant identification when you take or upload a photo in the Identify Plant feature. Photos sent to Plant.id are processed according to their privacy policy at plant.id. You can choose not to use the identification feature if you prefer not to share photos with Plant.id.

Notifications

Green Thumb may send local push notifications to remind you to care for your plants. These notifications are generated entirely on your device based on your care history. No notification data is transmitted to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app.

Children's Privacy

Green Thumb does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal information to use.

Data Deletion

All data stored by Green Thumb lives on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the Green Thumb app from your device. This permanently removes all plants, photos, care history, and preferences from your device.

If you have iCloud sync enabled, deleting the app will remove data from your device but may not immediately remove data from iCloud. To fully delete iCloud data, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Green Thumb and delete the associated data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
